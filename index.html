Who is Rodniel Briones?

Hello!, My name is Rodniel Briones, 25 years old living in Pagsabungan Mandaue City, Cebu, Philippines.
I am very passionate about programming and some other problem solving activities like Chess, and puzzles.

Unfortunately I did not finish college for a lot of reasons, but I really love coding so I decided to teach myself.
My goal in life is to become a "Self-Taught Fullstack Web & Andriod Developer" in the near future but for now I am currently studying the "Frontend".
