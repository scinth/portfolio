About Me

I am Rodniel Briones from Mandaue City Cebu, passionate about programming, loves any logical stuff that requires deep thinking, and is determined to become a professional Web Developer.

I am interested in anything related to programming, chess, self-improvement, psychology, nature, supernatural, mysteries, photo editing, problem solving, puzzles and other similar subjects.

It is my greatest desire to become an "Android and Fullstack Website Developer" to be able to contribute to the community by creating useful websites and apps that solves daily hassle and services that allows people to boost their productivity especially for students and self-taught.
